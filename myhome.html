WHO WE ARE?

FIND YOUR FIT is a platform for fitness seekers as well as trainers. It covers all those major services that a normal person needs to remain fit in daily life. Fitness hunters can hire personal trainer and will pay for taking a particular service for some fixed duration and trainers will get paid for their services.

Fitness Seekers

FIND YOUR FIT System is a portal that provides easiness to fitness seekers as well as fitness trainers. It provides individual portal for seekers where they can signup, become the member of website and can hire a personal trainer. On our platform, seekers can search trainer according to the service which they want to get and can submit query to admin.

Fitness Trainers

FIND YOUR FIT provides an opportunity to the trainers where they can register themselves and provide services. Trainer portal contains profiles of trainers. It contains their services along fee structure. Trainers will be paid for their services according to the fees structure. Therefore, it is a platform for trainers where they can earn handsom amount.

Frequently Asked Questions?

How can I hire trainer?

You can hire a personal trainer by signing up as a fitness seeker and by filling up a from. Admin will respond to you and give you contact number of trainer.

How can I register my self as trainer?

You can reister yourself by signing up as a fitness trainer and by filling up a from. Admin will respond to you and verify your cv or certifications.

How a trainer get paid?

Trainer will get paid by admin. Admin will be responsible for every transaction.

Can I choose trainer by myself or admin will suggest me?

Both options are available. If you want to choose trainer according to your choice then go to trainers page and search any trainer. Copy the name of that trainer and paste in the signup form field. Admin will provide you that trainer.

What happend if I left trainer'name field blank?

Admin will suggest you that trainer who is free according to your time.

Do admin charge for every service?

Yes admin will take 20% commission from every service.

Can fitness seeker quit service?

Yes, a fitness seeker can quit service but fee will not be refundable.
