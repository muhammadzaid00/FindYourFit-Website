Steps For Trainer Registration

Step:1

Step:2

Registration form will appear. Fill that form where all fields are mandatory and click on submit button.

Step:3

A confirmation email will send to your given email address by the admin.

Need Help?

Need help or have any query, Please contact directly to the admin. Contact details are mentioned in last section.

Steps For Hiring Trainer

Step:1

Go to Home Page and click on the Sign up as a Fitness Seeker button.

Step:2

Registration form will appear. Fill that form where all fields are mandatory except one and click on submit button.
